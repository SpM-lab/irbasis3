Other bases
===========

with any bases derived from :class:`sparse_ir.abstract.AbstractBasis`.

In particular, we allow working we offer a variant of the Discrete Lehmann
Representation: :class:`sparse_ir.dlr.DiscreteLehmannRepresentation`.
